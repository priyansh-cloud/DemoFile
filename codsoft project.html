Our Story

Founded in 2010, Savory Bites began as a small family-owned bistro with a passion for creating memorable dining experiences. Our journey started with just five tables and a dream to bring authentic flavors to our community.

Today, we continue that tradition with a commitment to using locally-sourced ingredients and innovative culinary techniques. Our award-winning chef brings over 20 years of international experience to every dish.

Chef Michael Rodriguez
